Lolo and the kid

An old hustler who often uses his young child to con wealthy people to make a living. One day, after a surprising encounter, he faces a predicament that decides the fate of their relationship.

Main Character

Lolo Mario- He was a man who no longer wanted to live his life. He was about to commit suicide when he heard a child's cry he stop it and didn't go on. He took care of the child.

Kid- A baby that Lolo found in the trash. His grandfather raised him and they robbed a wealthy family to live. The boy didn't study in school and lived on the street. The grandfather was born. Kid was adopted by a rich couple and he lived a good life.
