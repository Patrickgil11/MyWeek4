    • Heneral Luna

    This historical biopic focuses on General Antonio Luna, one of the fiercest generals of the Philippine Revolutionary Army, during the Philippine-American War. The film explores themes of patriotism, betrayal, and leadership.

    Main Character

    The main character of Heneral Luna is General Antonio Luna, portrayed by actor John Arcilla. General Luna was a highly skilled and passionate military leader during the Philippine-American War, known for his fiery temper and dedication to the cause of Philippine independence. The film centers around his leadership, struggles, and eventual assassination.

    Henry Francia, and Jerrold Tarog.

    Jerrold Tarog directed the film, which is based on historical events during the Philippine-American War and focuses on the life of General Antonio Luna.

    Content of the story

    General Luna, known for his strict discipline and fiery temperament, is appointed by President Emilio Aguinaldo to lead the revolutionary forces. Luna pushes for a united military strategy, believing that only a strong, centralized army can fend off the Americans. However, his aggressive leadership style and demand for obedience cause friction with other military leaders and government officials.
